About Jordan Stephenson

A full stack web developer from Denver, CO who is a whizz in Javascript. She has a a high passion for building creative quality websites

Coding has everything she loves: structure, analysis, language, logic, creativity and intellectual stimulation.

A self-proclaimed challenge-junkie, Jordan is always on the look out for new technologies to learn and new fourteeners to conquer with Gundy, her Shorthaired Pointer.
